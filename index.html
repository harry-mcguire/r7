Texture Fractions

Sand, Silt, & Clay Ratios

Soil physical matrices are categorized by explicit particle structural diameters: Sand (2.0–0.05 mm), Silt (0.05–0.002 mm), and Clay (<0.002 mm).

Agronomic Impact: Clays present high chemical storage arrays but compact readily. Sandy zones drain perfectly but cause extreme nutrient wash outs.
